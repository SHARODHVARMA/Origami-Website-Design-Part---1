Interesting Facts about Camel:-

1. Camel's ears are furry.

2. Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet.

3. When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Teddy Bear:-

1. The Teddy Bears' Picnic song was originally called The Teddy Bear Two Step.

2. The Oxford English Dictionary dates the first use of the term teddy bear to 1906.

3. There are about 2,000 speciality bear making stores in America! This does not count stores like Build-A-Bear.
